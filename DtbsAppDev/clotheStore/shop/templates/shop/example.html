About us

Huge selection

Internet-shop CamelRiderClub.kz - the largest in Kazakhstan catalog of fashionable men's clothes, shoes, accessories, perfumes and cosmetics, as well as sports goods.

Here you can pick up a basic men's closet for every day and decorate it with spectacular designer accessories.

Delivery with trying on

All the goods from the catalog of CamelRiderClub.kz you can try on before buying. Courier brings desirable models and sizes for choice, the buyer can pay for the goods after trying them on.

Payment and return

All the goods from the catalog CamelRiderClub.kz can be ordered without pre-payment. Upon delivery of the goods courier pay by credit card through mobile terminal or take cash from the buyer.
